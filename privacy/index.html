Privacy Policy

We are committed to ensuring that your personal information is protected. We do not collect or store personal identifying information or any details related to your traffic or browsing activity. However, during your use of our service, we may collect the following technical information:

  • Internet Service Provider (ISP)

This information is collected solely to improve our network performance and to provide you with the highest quality of service. It is analyzed in an aggregated and anonymous manner to help optimize server performance and maintain service quality.

2. How We Use Your Information
The information we collect is used exclusively to enhance and optimize our services, including the deployment and management of our servers. Please note that such data does not contain personally identifiable information and is only used to improve the quality and reliability of our service.

We take the security of your data very seriously. All data collected is stored securely, and we implement strict security measures to protect it from unauthorized access, disclosure, or alteration. Any third-party services we use are required to maintain appropriate security measures to protect your data.
